Arc Shot

A shot in which the subject is photographed by an encirling or moving camera

Interlude

A brief, intervening film scene or sequenee, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears within a film.

Prologue

A speech, prefaee, introduction, or brief scene preceding the the main action or plot of a film; contrast to epilogue
